Campus Information

Amaravati Campus

The Amaravati campus offers modern infrastructure with advanced labs, innovation centers, and engineering programs. It emphasizes research, technical excellence, and industry collaboration.

Amritapuri Campus

Located in Kerala near the Arabian Sea, this campus is known for excellence in Engineering, Biotechnology, and Computer Science. It promotes value-based education along with global research exposure.

Chennai Campus

The Chennai campus has strong industry collaboration and offers various engineering programs. Students benefit from internships and placements due to its proximity to major industries.

Coimbatore Campus

Administrative headquarters located in Ettimadai. It houses Schools of Engineering and Business with green surroundings, advanced research labs, and excellent placement records.

Nagercoil Campus

The Nagercoil campus focuses on arts, science, and foundational programs. It provides a peaceful academic environment with experienced faculty, modern classrooms, and strong academic guidance for holistic development.

Bangalore Campus

Situated in India's technology hub, this campus focuses on engineering, management, and technology programs with strong placement opportunities and startup exposure.
